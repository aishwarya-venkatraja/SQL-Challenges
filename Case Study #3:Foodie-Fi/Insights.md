# Insights and Recommendations

## Insights

1. Foodie-Fi serves 1,000 distinct customers, with 1,343 active subscriptions, indicating some customers have multiple subscriptions.
2. Approximately 83 people, on average, opt for the trial plan every month, indicating consistent interest in trying out the service.
3. Customers who complete the trial plan often prefer to upgrade to either the basic monthly or pro monthly plans, suggesting that these plans offer value and appeal.
4. Among the customer base, only 258 customers choose to upgrade to the pro annual plan. It takes an average of 104 days for customers to make this transition.
5. The overall churn rate for Foodie-Fi stands at 30.7%. Notably, 9.7% of customers churn right after their trial, indicating the need for improvements in the trial-to-paid conversion experience.
6. Customer churn primarily occurs after basic and pro monthly plans, while the pro annual plan, despite having fewer customers, generates high revenue and boasts the lowest churn rate.

## Recommendations

The two major areas that needs improvement are churning and Transitioning to Pro Annual plans.

**Churn rate reduction:**
1. Offer personalized recommendations to customers at different stages of their subscription journey, increasing engagement and satisfaction.
2. Solicit feedback from departing customers, especially those who churn after the trial. Use their insights to make targeted improvements to the trial experience and overall service.

**Increase Pro Annual Subscription:**

1. Since the pro annual plan has lower churn and generates substantial revenue, consider promoting it more aggressively. Highlight the cost savings and additional benefits of an annual commitment.
2. Educate customers about the advantages of annual plans, emphasizing the cost savings and uninterrupted access to content.
3. Offer discounts for customers opting Pro Annual plans.
